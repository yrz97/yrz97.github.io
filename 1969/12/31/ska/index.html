##Ska3 Phosphorylated by Cdk1 Binds Ndc80 and Recruits Ska to Kinetochores to Promote Mitotic Progression
[Ska]: spindle and kinetochore-associated protein complex,it has many microtubule-binding elements like ska1 and ska3 to promote kinetchore-microtubule attachmemnt
[Ndc80C]:Ndc80 complex, a core outer kinetochore component,a component of the KMN (Knl1/Mis12/Ndc80 complex) network
[Cdk1 phosphorylates]: this phosphorylation occurs specifically during mitosis and is required for the kinetochore localization of the Ska complex

Summary:

Ska protein complex is consists of two copies each of Ska1, Ska2, and Ska3 proteins. Here the author proposed Cdk1 phosphorylates Ska3 to promote its direct binding to the Ndc80C, then recruits the Ska complex to kinetochores where Ska1 can bind PP1 by its C-terminal domain and bind microtubules to promote anaphase onset.
